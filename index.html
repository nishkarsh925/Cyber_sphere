ViroGuard

ViroGuard is an online service that analyzes files and URLs for viruses, worms, trojans, and other kinds of malware. It aggregates results from various antivirus engines and tools to provide a comprehensive view of potential threats.

DeepFake Detecting

Deepfake detection refers to the methods and technologies used to identify and verify content that has been manipulated using deepfake techniques. Deepfakes use artificial intelligence, particularly deep learning, to create realistic-looking audio and video that can misrepresent reality—such as swapping faces in videos or generating synthetic voices.

IP Watchdog

IP Watchdog is a collaborative database that allows users to report and check IP addresses associated with malicious activities, such as hacking, spam, and abuse. It helps cybersecurity professionals, system administrators, and organizations identify potentially harmful IPs to protect their networks and systems.

INCIDENT RESPONSE

Incident response is the process of quickly and effectively addressing security incidents to minimize their impact on an organization.
